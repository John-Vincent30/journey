MY 2ND YEAR JOURNEY

I am Cris Jay Soliguen, a 2nd year student at Cagayan State University Solana-Lara Campus, taking up a Bachelor of Elementary Education. I've experienced my fair share of highs and lows.

Being a working student as a barber in Centro Solana hasn't been easy – juggling classes, work, and personal life can get overwhelming. There are days when I feel like I'm running on empty, but knowing that I'm doing it to support my family and secure my future keeps me going. I wasn't immune to setbacks, though – failing in midterms was a tough pill to swallow. But I've learned to pick myself up, dust myself off, and keep moving forward.

Throughout this journey, I've been blessed with an amazing support system. My family has been my rock, offering guidance and encouragement whenever I need it. My friends have also been incredible – we've navigated the ups and downs of college life together, and their camaraderie has made all the difference. We've shared laughs, tears, and late-night study sessions, and I'm grateful for every moment.

But what really keeps me motivated is my girlfriend, Lailanie. Even when I'm busy with work and struggling to keep up with my activities, she reminds me to stay focused and believes in me when I don't believe in myself. Her love and support inspire me to push through challenges and strive for excellence.

With their love and support, I'm determined to make the most of my college experience and overcome any obstacle that comes my way.
